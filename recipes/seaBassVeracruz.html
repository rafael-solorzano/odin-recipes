Sizzling Sea Bass Veracruz

Hailing from the Yucatan Peninsula, the Sea Bass Veracruz delivers a graceful mixture of buttery Sea Bass in a citrus, tomato-based sauce with olives, capers, and onions. Paired with a side of cilantro-lime rice, and avocado, each bite of Sea Bass is guaranteed to be a well balanced concotion of flavors.

Ingredients
  • Sea Bass filet
  • Onions
  • Capers
  • Olives
  • Tomato
  • Lime
  • White rice
  • Cilantro
  • Avocado
  • Butter
Steps
  1. Season SeaBass with lemon pepper seasoning mix
  2. Start a cup of white rice in a rice cooker
  3. In a sauce pan, mix diced tomato, olives, capers, and onions
  4. Once the sauce started to simmer, incorporate the butter and reduce the heat; gently stirring to prevent the butter from breaking.
  5. Once the Sea Bass has grilled marks, transfer it to the oven to finish cooking
  6. Mix the white rice with diced cilantro, and lime
  7. Plate the Sea Bass, top it with the tomato-based sauce and served with a side of cilantro-lime rice, and a side of avocado
