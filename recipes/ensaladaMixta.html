Ensalada Mixta

Ensalada mixta: Romaine lettuce with a cilantro ranch dressing, topped with grilled chickem, avocado, tomato, black beans, sweet corn, and crispy tortilla strips.

A refereshing salad with a burst of Latin flavors that satiates any savory craving, that is contrasted with a deliciously creamy cilantro ranch dressing. Complimnetned with seasonally fresh tomato, avocado, and sweet corn.

Ingredients
  • Chicken breast
  • Corn chip strips
  • sweet corn
  • black beans
  • avocado
  • tomato
  • romaine lettuce
  • cilantro ranch dressing
Steps
  1. wash romaine lettuce, and chop to desired size
  2. Mix chopped romaine lettuce with cilantro ranch dressing
  3. Season chicken with adobo seasoning mix, and grill on high heat
  4. top salad with sweet corn, black beans, toritllas strips, avocado, and tomato
  5. cut grilled chicken into bite-sized strips, and add to salad
